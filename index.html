ABOUT US

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO founded in 2020. It works towards Education, Women Empowerment, Animal Welfare, Environmental Conservation, and Community Development. The organization believes in collective action to create a better future for society.
